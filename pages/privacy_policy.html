Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Nov. 14th, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TheEndoInitiative collects, uses, and protects information when you use this website. This site is designed to respect your privacy and collects minimal data.


1. Information We Collect

This website does not collect personal information such as names, emails, accounts, or uploads.

The only data collected comes from Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is fully anonymous and does not identify you.

  • Approximate page views
  • Non-identifiable device or browser information
  • General location (region-level only, not city or IP)
  • Referring site (if any)

Cloudflare Analytics does not store IP addresses or personal identifiers.


2. Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, cross-site tracking, or advertising identifiers. Cloudflare may temporarily process technical information strictly for security (e.g., bot detection), but this is not stored in a personally identifiable way.


3. How We Use the Information

The anonymous data collected is used only for:

  • Understanding website traffic patterns
  • Improving layout and functionality
  • Detecting malicious traffic or attacks

No personal profiling, advertising, or sale of data occurs.


4. Third-Party Services

We rely on Cloudflare for hosting, security, and analytics. This may involve:

  • Temporary IP processing for security (not stored for analytics)
  • DDoS protection and firewall filtering
  • Anonymous traffic measurement

Cloudflare does not share or sell your information.


5. Links to Other Sites

This website may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy or security practices of those external pages. You should review their policies separately.


6. Data Security

This site uses Cloudflare’s global security features, including encryption, firewall protection, and bot management. No personal data is stored on our servers.


7. Children’s Privacy

This site does not target children and does not collect personal information. If you are under your local age of consent, you should use this site with the guidance of a guardian.


8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
